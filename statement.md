## Problem-Statement
### Problem
Educational institutions face significant challenges in managing student records, teacher information, and financial operations through manual, paper-based systems. These traditional methods lead to data inconsistency, time-consuming administrative tasks, financial tracking errors, and lack of real-time insights for decision-making. Schools struggle with inefficient fee collection, salary management, and comprehensive reporting, resulting in operational inefficiencies and poor resource utilization.
### Solution
A comprehensive School Management System that automates all major administrative functions including student enrollment, teacher management, financial operations, and analytical reporting. The system provides a centralized platform for efficient data management, real-time financial tracking, and insightful analytics to support informed decision-making.
